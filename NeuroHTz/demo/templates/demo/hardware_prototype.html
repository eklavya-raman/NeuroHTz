Hardware Prototype

Complete hardware design and component analysis

EEG Amplifier Module

ADS1299 - 8-Channel EEG AFE

High-precision analog front-end for EEG signal acquisition with low noise and high common-mode rejection.

  • Channels:8 differential
  • Resolution:24-bit ADC
  • Sample Rate:1-16 kSPS
  • Input Noise:1 µVpp
  • CMRR:110 dB
  • Power:5 mW/channel

Why chosen: Industry standard for medical EEG with excellent noise performance and integrated features.

Microcontroller Unit

ESP32-S3 - Dual Core MCU

Powerful microcontroller with WiFi/Bluetooth connectivity for real-time processing and data transmission.

  • Processor:Dual-core 240 MHz
  • Memory:512 KB SRAM
  • Storage:16 MB Flash
  • Connectivity:WiFi 6, BLE 5.0
  • ADC:12-bit, 20 channels
  • DSP:Hardware accelerated

Why chosen: Sufficient processing power for real-time filtering and wireless data transmission.

Audio Stimulus Generator

MAX98357A - I2S Audio Amplifier

High-quality audio amplifier for precise 40 Hz auditory stimulus generation with low distortion.

  • Output Power:3.2W @ 4Ω
  • THD+N:0.013% @ 1W
  • Frequency Response:20Hz - 20kHz
  • SNR:100 dB
  • Interface:I2S Digital
  • Gain Control:Software controlled

Why chosen: Excellent audio quality with precise frequency control essential for stimulus consistency.

Power Management

LT3045 - Ultra-Low Noise LDO

Ultra-low noise linear regulator ensuring clean power supply for sensitive analog circuits.

  • Output Voltage:3.3V, 5V rails
  • Noise Density:0.8 nV/√Hz
  • PSRR:79 dB @ 1 MHz
  • Dropout:300 mV @ 500 mA
  • Ripple Rejection:76 dB
  • Battery Life:8+ hours

Why chosen: Critical for maintaining signal integrity in sensitive EEG measurements.

Electrode Interface

Gold-plated Ag/AgCl Electrodes

Medical-grade electrodes with low impedance and stable interface for reliable EEG acquisition.

  • Material:Ag/AgCl with gold plating
  • Impedance:< 5 kΩ @ 10 Hz
  • Diameter:10 mm active area
  • Connector:DIN 42-802 standard
  • Drift:< 5 µV/hr
  • Configuration:19-channel 10-20 system

Why chosen: Medical standard with proven biocompatibility and stable electrical properties.

Data Storage & Transmission

32GB microSD + WiFi Module

Dual storage system with local backup and cloud synchronization for reliable data management.

  • Local Storage:32 GB microSD
  • Cloud Sync:Real-time upload
  • Data Rate:Up to 16 kSPS
  • Compression:Lossless (FLAC-like)
  • Encryption:AES-256
  • Backup:Automatic redundancy

Why chosen: Ensures data integrity with redundant storage and secure transmission protocols.
